About the app
Introduction

AJSearch stands for Article Journal Search. The app is used to search articles or journals for research purposes. Unlike other search engines, the app uses natural language processing and machine learning algorithms to find the top similar results for a given query. Also, the app shows a list of keywords that indicate the reason for matching results.

About version

The app is in its 1st version. There are some notes for this version as follows.

  • You can send the feedback for further development.
  • The app mainly focuses on topics in the Computer Science area.
